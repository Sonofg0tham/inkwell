# Inkwell Brand Identity

Direction: warm playful stationery — ink and paper, friendly not corporate

Fonts:
- Display: Fraunces (via Google Fonts, bundled locally as woff2 — use its soft/wonky character)
- Body: Atkinson Hyperlegible (accessible, designed for low-vision readers)
- Mono: IBM Plex Mono (model names and URLs only)

Palette (HSL):
- Background: 40 50% 97% (warm cream)
- Foreground: 230 30% 18% (ink navy)
- Accent: 16 85% 58% (warm coral — one accent only)
- Border: 35 30% 86%
- Muted: 35 25% 45%

Radius scale: 0.75rem controls, 1rem cards

Signature detail: "Blotty", a small ink-blob mascot whose face reflects status —
happy when connected, asleep when Inkwell is switched off, dizzy on a connection
error. Appears in the popup, options empty states, and the extension icon.

Scope note: the brand applies to the popup and options pages. The in-page overlay
(underlines and the suggestion card) stays neutral and unobtrusive; underline
colours are semantic (red/amber/blue), not brand colours.

Reserved for this product. Not sonofg0tham. Not any other product Craig has built.
